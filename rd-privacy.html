Ripple Dripple — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Information We Collect

Ripple Dripple collects your email address for account creation and your XRP Ledger wallet address when you connect via Xaman. We do not store private keys or seed phrases.

How We Use Your Information

Your wallet address is used to display your staking balance, calculate rewards, and process deposit/withdrawal requests on the XRP Ledger.

Data Storage

Account data is stored on secure servers operated by Campione Infrastructure LLC.

Third Party Services

We use Xaman (formerly XUMM) for wallet authentication. Their privacy policy applies to their service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.
